Welcome to "How Much Money Do I Have?"

Our app is designed to give you a clear, straightforward answer to one simple question: How much money do I have right now? We understand that sometimes you don't want to deal with complex financial trackers, adjust amounts, or view complicated graphics about spending or saving. That's why our app skips all that. It’s easy to use—just enter the amount of euros or pounds you have, and our app will tell you your total. There’s no need for labels or categories. Want to know how much you have in another currency? Currently, our app supports only euros and pounds, but we're looking to include more currencies in the future. Use our app when you need a quick and easy way to see your total funds without any hassle. Start now and keep it simple!

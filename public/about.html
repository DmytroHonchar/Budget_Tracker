About Pocket

Features

  • Quick Totals: Instantly see your total amount of money in euros or pounds.
  • Simple Input: Just enter your amounts—no need for detailed categories or tags.
  • User-Friendly Interface: My interface is designed to be intuitive and super easy to use.
  • Secure: Your financial data is securely stored and only accessible to you.

How It Works

  1. Register: Create an account with your email and set a secure password.
  2. Log In: Enter your login details to access your account.
  3. Enter Amounts: Input the amounts you have in euros or pounds.
  4. See Your Total: Instantly view your total money in a clear and understandable manner.

About Me

I like to keep things simple. My mission is to provide a tool that makes it easy for you to quickly see how much money you have, without the stress and complexity of traditional financial trackers.
